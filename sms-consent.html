Consent to Receive SMS Text Messages

By pressing 2 during our automated phone system, you expressly consent to receive SMS text messages from Georgia Apostille & Notary at the phone number you provide. These messages will relate to your notary and apostille service inquiry. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message.

How Consent is Collected

Georgia Apostille & Notary collects explicit SMS messaging consent through our automated phone system (IVR). The consent process works as follows:

  1. The caller dials our business phone number.
  2. The caller selects their preferred language (English or Spanish).
  3. The caller hears the following message:

English IVR Script:

"We will send you a message with a few questions about your documents so we can get started right away. You can reply at your convenience and we typically respond within seconds. To receive your message via WhatsApp, press 1. By pressing 2, you consent to receive SMS text messages from Georgia Apostille and Notary. Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP. To receive a text message, press 2."

Spanish IVR Script:

"Le enviaremos un mensaje con algunas preguntas sobre sus documentos para comenzar de inmediato. Puede responder cuando le sea conveniente y normalmente respondemos en segundos. Para recibir su mensaje por WhatsApp, oprima 1. Al oprimir 2, usted acepta recibir mensajes de texto SMS de Georgia Apostille and Notary. Se pueden aplicar tarifas de mensajes y datos. Puede cancelar en cualquier momento respondiendo STOP. Para recibir un mensaje de texto, oprima 2."
  1. The caller actively presses 2 to opt in to receiving SMS text messages.
  2. The caller confirms the phone number where they want to receive the text message.

No SMS message is sent unless the caller actively presses 2 after hearing the consent disclosure. This constitutes explicit, informed consent to receive SMS text messages from Georgia Apostille & Notary.

Types of Messages

SMS messages sent by Georgia Apostille & Notary are limited to:

  • Document intake questions related to the client's notary or apostille service request
  • Service information and pricing estimates
  • Appointment scheduling and confirmation
  • Document status updates

We do not send marketing messages, promotional content, or share your number with third parties for advertising purposes.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on the client's service needs and the ongoing conversation. Typically, clients receive between 5 and 20 messages per service interaction.

How to Opt Out

You can opt out of receiving SMS text messages at any time by replying STOP to any message received from Georgia Apostille & Notary. Upon receiving a STOP request, we will confirm your opt-out and no further SMS messages will be sent to your phone number.

You may also opt out by contacting us directly at (404) 852-6970 or ga.apostille.notary@gmail.com and requesting removal from SMS messaging.

How to Get Help

Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact us at (404) 852-6970 or ga.apostille.notary@gmail.com.
